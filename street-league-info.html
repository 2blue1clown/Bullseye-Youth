Street League

League Details

  • Where:Wembly Oval
  • When:Games are on Monday night and go for 1 hour starting at 6:30pm or 7:30pm
  • Cost: Approximately $45 per person depending on numbers in the team

The league is organized by Marlow Street Ultimate Club who do a fantasic job of supporting a super inclusive and welcoming culture

What are the games like?

At Street League (and in frisbee in general) teams do their best to get along with one another and make sure no-one gets hurt. The league is primarily social rather than competitive so it is a great place for new people to get into frisbee.
