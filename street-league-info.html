Street League

Details

  • Where: Wembley Sports Oval
  • When: Games are either in the 6:30-7:30 or 7:30-8:30 timeslot. The exact start dates are still to be finalized, but they will be soon.
  • Cost: Approximately $45 per person for the whole season, depending on numbers.

The league is organized by Marlow Street Ultimate Club, who do a fantastic job of creating a super inclusive and welcoming culture.

What are the games like?

Street League is primarily social rather than competitive, so it is a great place for new people to get into Ultimate. Even though everyone is trying their hardest, players always do their best to get along with one another and make sure to always be safe.
