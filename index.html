Club Youth Frisbee is here!

Bullseye Ultimate is a Club committed to growing the opportunities for young frisbee players in Perth.

Who are we?

Since our club began in ?2014? we put culture and the spirit of the game first. Our men's team won the Spirit of theGame Award for the Division 2 Australian Ultimate Championships two years in a row in 2018 and 2019. The focus on safe play and calm discussions is championed by our club.

The Perth Ultimate scene as been gradually growing over the past 20 years and we think it is our role to give young frisbee players an opportunity to play and improve in our favorite sport.

The club has been partnering with Shenton College to provide some Ultimate coaching after school, and we stoked to have a place for our young members test their skills

Street League Ultimate

Street League is an Ultimate Frisbee league that started up earlier this year because a group of super passionate people thought they could run a league better than what already existed in Perth. And they were right! They are now an incorporated club and ready to include youth in their league.

Jono Jones and Ben Guadin are coaches that have been providing Youth Frisbee at Shenton since the start of 2021 and will be the Captains/Coaches at these games providing coaching and making sure that everything is running smoothly.

To help keep the team competitive we will have some Bullseye Senior members playing too so that our team has a competitive chance.

  • When: Games are either in either in the 6:30 - 7:30 or 7:30 to 8:30 time slot. The exact start dates are still to be 100% finalized but it will be soon.
  • Where: Wembly Oval
  • Cost: approx. $45 depending on numbers
