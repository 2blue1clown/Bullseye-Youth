BULLSEYE YOUTH

Club Youth Frisbee is here!

Bullseye Ultimate is a Club committed to growing the opportunities for young frisbee players in Perth Since our club began in ?2014? put culture and the spirit of the game first. Our men's team won the Spirit of the Game Award for the Division 2 Australian Ultimate Championships two years in a row in 2018 and 2019. The focus on safety and calm discussions is championed by our club. The Perth Ultimate scene as been gradually growing over the past 20 years and we think it is our role to give young frisbee players an opportunity to play and improve in our favorite sport. The club has been partnering with Shenton College to provide some Ultimate coaching after schools, and now have a place for our young members to test their skills and compete.

Street League Ultimate

Street League is an Ultimate Frisbee league that started up earlier this year because a group of super passionate people thought they could run a league better than what already existed in Perth. And they were right! They are now incorporated and now ready to include youth in what is a league that primarily consists of people in their 20s playing frisbee as a social sport. Jono Jones and Ben Graudin are coaches that have been providing Youth Frisbee at Shenton since the start of 2021 and will be the Captains/Coaches at these games providing coaching and making sure that everything is running smoothly.
